Our Story

A bank built by people, for people

NovaBank started as a small community bank in 1989 with a single branch and a simple promise: treat every customer like family. Three decades later, we serve over 2.4 million customers across 180+ countries — and we still keep that promise.

From pioneering mobile banking in our region to launching one of the most secure digital wallets in the industry, we constantly reinvent banking around what truly matters: trust, simplicity and your goals.
